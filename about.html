About me

After completing a Bachelor's degree in English studies with a specialization in French as a Foreign Language (FLE), I worked for nearly ten years as an English teacher in France (middle school, vocational training centers and private lessons).

Today, I teach French as a foreign language to learners from all over the world.

I am passionate about travelling and I also enjoy learning new languages myself.

I speak French, English and Italian fluently, and I also have basic knowledge of French Sign Language and Arabic.

How does a lesson work?

  • 🗣 Conversation adapted to your level
  • ✏️ Corrections and advice to help you progress
  • 📚 Useful vocabulary for everyday life
  • 📖 Grammar explanations when needed
  • 🎯 Goal: speak more naturally and with confidence
